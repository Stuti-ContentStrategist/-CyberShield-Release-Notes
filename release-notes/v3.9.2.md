# v3.9.2

**Release Date:** November 12, 2024\
**Audience:** Internal Teams & Developers\
**Type:** Minor Version Update

***

#### **Summary**

Version 3.9.2 focuses on enhancing regulatory compliance and strengthening security. Updates include improved data handling, audit logging, encryption standards, and multi-factor authentication policies to ensure safer and more compliant system operations.

***

#### **Regulatory Compliance Updates**

* Updated data handling procedures to align with GDPR requirements.
* Introduced automated audit logs to meet SOC 2 Type II standards.
* Enhanced encryption algorithms for compliance with PCI DSS guidelines.

***

#### **Security Enhancements**

* Strengthened MFA token validation for all users.
* Improved endpoint encryption key rotation policies.
